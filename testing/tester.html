How does this work?

  • Your browser location is determined by the W3C Geolocation API
  • freegeoip.net is used to find the locations of websites and IP addresses
  • The flag icons are by GoSquared
  • The maps are by Mapbox
  • Addresses from latitude and longitude are provided by Nominatum (OSM)
